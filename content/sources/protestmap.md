This interactive map displays locations of American Indian Movement (AIM) protests and chapter locations from 1970-1975. Red markers indicate protest sites and significant events, while blue markers show AIM chapter locations across the United States. Click markers for details about each location, and use the layer control to toggle between protests and chapters.
